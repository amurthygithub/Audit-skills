# Industry: Public Sector (government audits, Yellow Book / GAO)

## Posture
Government audits under Government Auditing Standards (Yellow Book / GAO). Single Audit Act compliance for entities expending federal funds. Additional reporting on internal control and compliance. Performance audits in addition to financial audits.

## Workpaper Standards
AICPA AU-C 230 (audit documentation) for non-issuer audits. Government Auditing Standards (GAGAS) Chapter 5 for quality control and peer review. OMB Uniform Guidance (2 CFR 200) for single audits.

## Typical Workpapers
- Schedule of Expenditures of Federal Awards (SEFA)
- Internal control over compliance testing
- Compliance testing for major programs
- Audit findings in 5-part C-C-C-E-R format with questioned costs
- Management representation letter covering federal awards

## Pain Points
- Single audit major program determination thresholds
- Compliance supplement updates (annual OMB release)
- Questioned costs quantification and reporting
- Yellow Book CPE requirements for auditors (80 hours/2 years)
- Dual-reporting requirements (financial audit + compliance audit)

## Top Use Cases
- UC-02: 5-part finding for control gap
