# Chunk 06 — Output Templates, Electronic WP, Review

## Standard Workpaper Template

Header: CLIENT, PERIOD, WP INDEX, PREPARED BY, REVIEWED BY.
Body: OBJECTIVE, SOURCE, ASSERTION(S), PROCEDURE, DATA/ANALYSIS, TICKMARK LEGEND, CONCLUSION, EXCEPTIONS, CROSS-REFS.
Footer: REVIEW NOTES (date, reviewer, note, resolution).

## Sampling Workpaper Template

Section A PLANNING: objective, population (N=), sampling unit, approach (statistical/nonstat), method, tolerable misstatement/rate, expected misstatement/rate, risk level, confidence level, required sample size (formula shown).

Section B SELECTION: selection method, random start, sampling interval, items selected.

Section C PERFORMANCE: procedure applied, exceptions/misstatements found (id, description, amount, cause, type).

Section D EVALUATION: sample deviation rate/misstatement, projection method, projected misstatement, ULM (for MUS), comparison to tolerable, sampling risk assessment.

Section E QUALITATIVE: nature of misstatements, cause analysis, relationship to other work.

Section F CONCLUSION: conclusion, impact on risk assessment, cross-references.

## Finding Documentation Template

FINDING #, WP INDEX, SEVERITY, ASSERTION. 5-part: CONDITION, CRITERIA, CAUSE, EFFECT, RECOMMENDATION. Recommended: MANAGEMENT RESPONSE, FOLLOW-UP.

## Engagement Completion Document (AS 1215.13)

1. Significant findings/issues (8 categories per AS 1215.12).
2. Evaluation of uncorrected misstatements.
3. Overall conclusion.
4. Independence compliance.
5. Review completion (partner, EQR, documentation completion date <=14 days after report release).

## IPE Validation Log Template

Field: WP Index, Description of IPE, Source System & Report Name, Purpose of Use, Completeness Procedures, Accuracy Procedures, ITGC Reference, Standard Tickmarks Used, Conclusion.

## Electronic Workpaper Requirements

Access Controls: password protection, access logs, role-based access, unauthorized access prevention (firm quality control policies per QC 1000; see AS 1215.16-.17 for post-completion documentation requirements).
Version Control: prevent unauthorized modification after completion, audit trail of all modifications, no deletion after completion date (AS 1215.17).
Digital Signatures: electronic sign-off with authentication, attributable to individual, tamper-resistant timestamps.
Retention: 7-year retention from report release (AS 1215.14), maintain readability, format migration plans, backup/disaster recovery. PDF/A (ISO 19005) recommended for archival.

## Review Standards

### Supervisory Review (AS 1201)
All workpapers reviewed by supervisory team member before report release. Reviewer must have appropriate competence and authority.

### Engagement Partner Review (AS 1215.15)
Complete before report release. Partner reviews significant judgments, risk assessments, and conclusions. All necessary procedures complete before sign-off.

### Engagement Quality Review — EQR (AS 1220)
Required for listed issuer audits and other engagements per firm policy. EQR reviewer must be qualified partner NOT on engagement team. Evaluate significant judgments and conclusions. Must not be overridden by engagement team.

### Review Notes Documentation
Every note: date, reviewer, WP index, description, resolution, clearance.
