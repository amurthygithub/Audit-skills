# Limits & Disclaimers -- audit-workpapers

## 1. What this skill is NOT

- Not a substitute for professional judgment. Always have a qualified auditor review outputs.
- Not legal advice. PCAOB, AICPA, ISA, and ISACA standards are interpreted by practitioners, regulators, and courts.
- Not a certification. Using the skill does not confer CPA, CISA, or any other credential.
- Not an audit opinion. The skill helps produce workpapers; the auditor signs the opinion.
