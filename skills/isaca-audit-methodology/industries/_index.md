## How industries are modeled

1. **Posture** - typical audit context, regulatory environment, common frameworks
2. **Boundary** - what is the audit scope in this industry
3. **Inheritance pattern** - how controls cascade or inherit from parent orgs/vendors
4. **Regulator/customer relationship** - key oversight bodies and reporting
5. **Top use cases** - linked use-case files
6. **Pain points** - common auditor and auditee challenges
7. **References** - industry-specific guidance
